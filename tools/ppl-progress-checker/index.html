The six FAR 61.109 requirements

For an airplane single-engine land private pilot certificate, FAR 61.109(a) sets these minimums:

  • 40 hours total flight time
  • 20 hours of dual (flight training from a CFI)
  • 10 hours of solo flight
  • 5 hours of solo cross-country
  • 3 hours of night training (plus a night cross-country over 100 NM and 10 night takeoffs/landings at a towered airport)
  • 3 hours of instrument training

For the full breakdown, read FAR 61.109 explained and the night flight requirements. When you think you are close, run the checkride readiness checklist.
