Free tools for student pilots

No signup, no tracking — quick calculators for the questions every student pilot asks. They run entirely in your browser.

Home›Tools
These tools are estimates, not regulatory determinations. SoloTrack is a logbook tool, not a regulator. Always confirm currency and certification requirements with your CFI and the current Federal Aviation Regulations.
FAR 61.57

Currency Calculator

Am I current to carry passengers? Estimate day, night, and instrument currency from your recent landings and approaches.
FAR 61.109

PPL Progress Checker

See which of the six private-pilot aeronautical-experience requirements you have met and how many hours remain.
FAR 61.56

Flight Review Calculator

Enter your last flight review date and see when the next one is due — the end of the 24th calendar month.
Checkride

Checkride Readiness Checklist

An interactive checklist of the FAR 61.109(a) requirements. Your progress saves in your browser.

Track all of this automatically

These calculators answer one question at a time. SoloTrack keeps the answers up to date for you — logging flights once and watching your currency, PPL progress, and flight-review window update in real time. Learn more in Currency & PPL progress.
