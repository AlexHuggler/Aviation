How the flight review window works

FAR 61.56 requires that, to act as pilot in command, you must have completed a flight review within the preceding 24 calendar months. The key word is calendar: a review counts through the end of the month, not the exact day. A flight review completed on June 15, 2024 keeps you legal to act as PIC through June 30, 2026.

A satisfactory practical test (a checkride) for a new certificate or rating, or certain proficiency programs, can also satisfy the requirement. This tool only does the date math on the value you enter — it does not know what kind of event it was.

Stop doing this math by hand
